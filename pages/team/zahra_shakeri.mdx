Zahra Shakeri is an Assistant Professor of Health Informatics and Information Visualization at the Dalla Lana School of Public Health, University of Toronto. She received her PhD in Computer Science from the University of Calgary and was a
Postdoctoral Fellow at [Gehlenborg Lab](http://gehlenborglab.org/) in the Department of Biomedical Informatics at Harvard Medical School. Dr. Shakeri's research centers on health informatics, information visualization, precision (public) health, digital health, and social media analysis.
