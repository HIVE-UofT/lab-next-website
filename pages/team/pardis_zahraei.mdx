Pardis Sadat Zahraei is a 4th-year undergraduate student pursuing Computer Engineering at Sharif University of Technology. Computational Linguistics, Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning are fields that deeply fascinate her.
An area that captivates Pardis is challenging and evaluating large language models. She loves digging into the data to challenge state-of-the-art language models, figuring out where they struggle and what biases they might have. Her goal is to help create more ethical, trustworthy AI assistants that can truly benefit society.
Beyond her academic interests, she is an avid reader of fantasy novels and a devoted fan of the Lord of the Rings series. She is always enthusiastic about discussing the latest breakthroughs in AI and dreaming up ways to push the boundaries even further.
